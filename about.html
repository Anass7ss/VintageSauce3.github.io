Our Services

At Vintage Sauce, we are committed to providing more than just vintage clothing. We offer a range of services to ensure that your experience with us is exceptional and memorable. Explore our services below:

Clothing Repairs & Restoration

Vintage clothing sometimes needs a little TLC, and we're here for it! Our repair and restoration services are perfect for breathing new life into worn-out garments. From fixing broken zippers to restoring damaged fabric, we take great care in preserving the quality and integrity of each piece.

Free delivery

We believe that shopping for vintage should be as convenient as it is exciting! That's why we offer free delivery on all orders within Morocco. Whether you're purchasing one item or curating an entire wardrobe, you can enjoy the ease of having your vintage finds delivered straight to your door—at no extra cost.

Personal Styling Consultations

Looking for the perfect vintage outfit ? Our expert stylists are here to help! We offer personalized styling consultations to guide you in choosing pieces that complement your unique style. Whether you're dressing for a special occasion or looking to refresh your wardrobe, we'll help you create the perfect look using our curated selection of vintage clothing.
